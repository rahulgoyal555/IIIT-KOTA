Introduction

Indian Institute of Information Technology Kota (IIIT, Kota) is one of the Indian Institutes of Information Technology proposed to be located at Ranpur near Kota, Rajasthan. The academic session of IIIT Kota started from its temporary campus at Malaviya National Institute of Technology Jaipur from year 2013.[1] IIIT Kota is a joint venture of the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India with Industries in Public-Private Partnership model.
